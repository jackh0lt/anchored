Anchored

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version: Anchored doesn't collect your personal data. Your tasks stay on your device and in your iCloud account. That's it. Everything below is just the longer version of that same sentence.

What Anchored stores and where

Everything you put into Anchored — your tasks, lists, folders, Brain Dump items, reminders, and settings — is stored in two places only:

On your device, using local storage. And in your personal iCloud account, if you're signed in with an Apple ID.

Your iCloud data is governed by Apple's privacy policy. Anchored never sees it, touches it, or has access to it. It goes directly between your device and Apple's servers.

What Anchored does not do

  • Collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • Track your location
  • Sell your data — to anyone, ever
  • Use advertising SDKs
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Run a server that receives your task data

Crash reports

If your device is opted into sharing diagnostics with developers — a setting you control in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements — Apple may share anonymous crash reports when the app crashes. These contain technical information about the crash only. No task content, no personal information.

You can turn this off at any time in your device settings.

Shared lists (Anchored Pro)

If you use shared lists, the content of those lists is shared with your collaborators via CloudKit — Apple's own sharing infrastructure. Anchored doesn't intermediate this. Your collaborators see what you add to a shared list, and you see what they add. This is the intended behavior of the feature.

Purchases

If you purchase Anchored Pro, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. Anchored never sees your payment information.

Your data is yours

You can export all of your data at any time from Settings → Export Data. You can delete everything by deleting the app. If you use iCloud sync, you can also remove the iCloud data from Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage on your device.

We will never hold your data hostage. It was yours before you opened Anchored and it stays yours after.

Children

Anchored is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If anything here changes — which we'd only do if the app changes in a meaningful way — we'll update the date at the top of this page. We won't bury changes in legalese or send you a 47-page update to scroll past.
