Privacy Policy
Anchored — Your tasks, the way you think
Last updated: June 2026

The short version
Anchored doesn't collect your personal data. Your tasks stay on your device and in your iCloud account. That's it.

What Anchored stores and where
Everything you put into Anchored — your tasks, lists, folders, Brain Dump items, reminders, and settings — is stored in two places only:

On your device, using local storage
In your personal iCloud account, if you're signed in with an Apple ID

Your iCloud data is governed by Apple's privacy policy. Anchored never sees it, touches it, or has access to it. It goes directly between your device and Apple's servers.

What Anchored does not do

Does not collect your name, email, or any personal information
Does not track your location
Does not sell your data — to anyone, ever
Does not use advertising SDKs
Does not share your data with third parties
Does not have a server that receives your task data


Crash reports
If your device is opted into sharing diagnostics with developers (a setting you control in iOS Settings → Privacy → Analytics), Apple may share anonymous crash reports with us when the app crashes. These reports contain technical information about the crash — no task content, no personal information.
You can turn this off at any time in your device settings.

Shared lists (Anchored Pro)
If you use shared lists, the content of those lists is shared with your collaborators via CloudKit — Apple's sharing infrastructure. Anchored does not intermediate this. Your collaborators see what you add to a shared list, and you see what they add. This is the intended behavior of the feature.

Purchases
If you purchase Anchored Pro, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. Anchored never sees your payment information.

Your data is yours
You can export all of your data at any time from Settings → Export Data. You can delete all of your data by deleting the app. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete the iCloud data from Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage on your device.

Children
Anchored is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy
If anything here changes — which we'd only do if the app changes in a meaningful way — we'll update the date at the top of this page. We won't bury changes in legalese.
